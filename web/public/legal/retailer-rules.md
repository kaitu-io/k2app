# 分销商计划 / Retailer Program

**最后更新 / Last Updated: 2025-11-28**

---

## 中文版本

### 一、计划理念

1.1 开途不设立销售岗位，但我们深知优质的推广对于产品传播的重要性。因此，我们推出了分销商计划，让每一位认可开途的用户都能成为我们的合作伙伴。

1.2 我们相信，真正热爱产品的人才能做出最好的推广。分销商计划的核心是「共同成长」——您的付出会得到相应的回报，您的成长会带来更高的收益。

1.3 让投入成本的人可以获得高复利收入，真正成为合伙人。我们为持续投入、将分销作为重要收入来源的伙伴提供完整的复利模式，让每一份努力都能带来长期价值。

1.4 我们提供完善的追踪系统，确保每一次有效推荐都能被准确记录和结算。

### 二、等级体系

开途分销商计划采用四级成长体系，您可以通过持续的努力获得更高的分成比例和更多的权益。

#### L1 推荐者

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 首单分成 | 20% |
| 续费分成 | 无 |
| 升级条件 | 发送邮件至 partner@kaitu.io 申请 |

**说明**：这是入门级别，任何人都可以通过邮件申请成为推荐者。您推荐的用户首次付款时，您将获得 20% 的分成。

#### L2 分销商

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 首单分成 | 25% |
| 续费分成 | 10% |
| 升级条件 | 累计带来 10 个付费用户 |

**说明**：当您累计推荐 10 个付费用户后，自动升级为分销商。从此刻起，您不仅能获得更高的首单分成，还能享受用户续费带来的持续收益。

#### L3 优质分销商

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 首单分成 | 30% |
| 续费分成 | 20% |
| 升级条件 | 累计带来 30 个付费用户 + 提交内容创作证明 |

**说明**：优质分销商需要展示您的推广能力和内容创作实力。请提交您的推广渠道、创作内容等相关资料，经审核通过后即可升级。

#### L4 合伙人

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 首单分成 | 30% |
| 续费分成 | 30%（完整复利） |
| 升级条件 | 累计带来 100 个付费用户 + 持续活跃的内容运营 |

**说明**：合伙人是我们最高级别的分销商。当您证明自己在持续投入、将分销作为重要的收入来源时，我们将为您开启完整的复利模式——您推荐的每一位用户的每一笔订单，都将为您带来 30% 的分成。

### 三、重要规则

3.1 **等级变更生效**：当您的等级提升后，您名下所有用户的后续订单都将按新的分成比例计算。

3.2 **累计计算**：付费用户数量为累计值，不会因时间重置。每一次成功的推荐都会计入您的成长记录。

3.3 **内容证明**：L3 及以上等级需要提交内容创作证明，包括但不限于：
- 自媒体账号（YouTube、Twitter、微博、公众号等）
- 发布的推广内容链接
- 社区运营记录
- 其他能证明您推广能力的材料

3.4 **审核机制**：等级升级申请将在 3 个工作日内完成审核，结果通过邮件通知。

3.5 **等级降级**：以下情况可能导致等级降级：
- **长期不活跃**：L3/L4 等级分销商连续 6 个月无新付费用户，将降一级
- **内容证明失效**：L3/L4 等级分销商的自媒体账号停更超过 3 个月，将降级
- **违规行为**：违反推广规范或存在作弊行为，将由管理员手动降级
- **推广竞品**：推广竞争对手产品的力度超过推广开途，将直接取消分销商资格

### 四、结算条款

4.1 **实时到账**：分成奖励实时计入钱包余额，无需等待结算周期。

4.2 **提现条件**：
- 钱包余额达到最低提现金额 **30 美元**
- 账户状态正常，无违规记录

4.3 **提现方式**：支持提现到主流数字货币钱包：
- USDT（TRC20/ERC20 网络）
- USDC（ERC20 网络）
- 其他主流币种（以实际支持为准）

4.4 **处理时间**：提现申请将在 1-3 个工作日内处理。

### 五、用户奖励

5.1 **双向奖励**：除分销商获得分成外，被推荐的新用户在首次购买时也会获得额外的会员时长奖励。

5.2 **奖励发放**：用户奖励在订单支付成功后自动发放到用户账户。

### 六、分销商责任

6.1 **合规推广**：分销商必须遵守以下推广规范：
- 不得进行虚假宣传或夸大服务功能
- 不得使用垃圾邮件、恶意软件等方式推广
- 不得冒充开途官方或误导用户
- 不得在禁止 VPN 服务的地区进行推广

6.2 **内容真实**：所有推广内容必须真实、准确，不得包含误导性信息。

6.3 **用户隐私**：不得收集、存储或滥用通过推广获取的用户信息。

6.4 **违规处理**：违反上述规定可能导致：
- 分成奖励被取消或扣除
- 分销商等级被降级
- 分销商资格被暂停或终止
- 账户被封禁

### 七、规则变更

7.1 **修改权利**：开途保留随时修改本分销商规则的权利。

7.2 **变更通知**：规则变更将通过邮件或网站公告通知分销商。

7.3 **继续参与**：规则变更后继续参与分销商计划，即表示接受新规则。

### 八、联系方式

如果您对分销商计划有任何疑问，或想申请成为分销商，请联系我们：

**电子邮件**：partner@kaitu.io

## English Version

### 1. Program Philosophy

1.1 Kaitu does not have dedicated sales positions, but we understand the importance of quality promotion for product growth. Therefore, we launched the Retailer Program, allowing every user who believes in Kaitu to become our partner.

1.2 We believe that people who truly love the product can create the best promotions. The core of our Retailer Program is "growing together" — your efforts will be rewarded accordingly, and your growth will bring higher returns.

1.3 We enable those who invest resources to earn high compound returns and truly become partners. We provide a complete compound model for partners who continuously invest and treat distribution as a major income source, ensuring every effort brings long-term value.

1.4 We provide a comprehensive tracking system to ensure every valid referral is accurately recorded and settled.

### 2. Tier System

The Kaitu Retailer Program uses a four-tier growth system. You can earn higher commission rates and more benefits through continuous effort.

#### L1 Referrer

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| First Order Commission | 20% |
| Renewal Commission | None |
| Upgrade Requirement | Email partner@kaitu.io to apply |

**Description**: This is the entry level. Anyone can become a Referrer by sending an email application. You will earn 20% commission when your referred users make their first payment.

#### L2 Retailer

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| First Order Commission | 25% |
| Renewal Commission | 10% |
| Upgrade Requirement | Accumulated 10 paying users |

**Description**: Once you have referred 10 paying users in total, you will automatically upgrade to Retailer. From this point, you will not only earn higher first-order commissions but also enjoy continuous income from user renewals.

#### L3 Premium Retailer

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| First Order Commission | 30% |
| Renewal Commission | 20% |
| Upgrade Requirement | Accumulated 30 paying users + Submit content creation proof |

**Description**: Premium Retailers need to demonstrate their promotional capabilities and content creation skills. Please submit your promotional channels, created content, and other relevant materials for review.

#### L4 Partner

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| First Order Commission | 30% |
| Renewal Commission | 30% (Full compound) |
| Upgrade Requirement | Accumulated 100 paying users + Continuous active content operation |

**Description**: Partner is our highest retailer tier. When you prove that you are continuously investing and treating distribution as an important income source, we will enable full compound mode for you — every order from every user you refer will earn you 30% commission.

### 3. Important Rules

3.1 **Tier Change Effectiveness**: When your tier is upgraded, all subsequent orders from users under your account will be calculated at the new commission rate.

3.2 **Cumulative Calculation**: The number of paying users is cumulative and does not reset over time. Every successful referral counts toward your growth record.

3.3 **Content Proof**: L3 and above tiers require submission of content creation proof, including but not limited to:
- Social media accounts (YouTube, Twitter, Weibo, WeChat Official Account, etc.)
- Published promotional content links
- Community operation records
- Other materials that can prove your promotional capabilities

3.4 **Review Process**: Tier upgrade applications will be reviewed within 3 business days, with results notified via email.

3.5 **Tier Demotion**: The following situations may result in tier demotion:
- **Long-term Inactivity**: L3/L4 retailers with no new paying users for 6 consecutive months will be demoted one tier
- **Content Proof Expiration**: L3/L4 retailers whose social media accounts have been inactive for over 3 months will be demoted
- **Violations**: Violating promotional guidelines or engaging in fraudulent behavior will result in manual demotion by administrators
- **Promoting Competitors**: Promoting competitor products more actively than Kaitu will result in immediate termination of retailer status

### 4. Settlement Terms

4.1 **Real-time Credit**: Commission rewards are credited to your wallet balance in real-time, no waiting period required.

4.2 **Withdrawal Conditions**:
- Wallet balance reaches minimum withdrawal amount of **$30 USD**
- Account is in good standing with no violations

4.3 **Withdrawal Methods**: Withdrawals are supported to mainstream cryptocurrency wallets:
- USDT (TRC20/ERC20 networks)
- USDC (ERC20 network)
- Other mainstream coins (subject to actual support)

4.4 **Processing Time**: Withdrawal requests are processed within 1-3 business days.

### 5. User Rewards

5.1 **Two-Way Rewards**: In addition to retailer commissions, referred new users also receive extra membership time rewards on their first purchase.

5.2 **Reward Distribution**: User rewards are automatically credited to user accounts after successful order payment.

### 6. Retailer Responsibilities

6.1 **Compliant Promotion**: Retailers must follow these promotional guidelines:
- No false advertising or exaggerating service features
- No spam, malware, or other malicious promotion methods
- No impersonating Kaitu official or misleading users
- No promoting in regions where VPN services are prohibited

6.2 **Content Accuracy**: All promotional content must be truthful and accurate, without misleading information.

6.3 **User Privacy**: Do not collect, store, or misuse user information obtained through promotions.

6.4 **Violation Handling**: Violations of the above may result in:
- Commission rewards being cancelled or deducted
- Retailer tier being downgraded
- Retailer status being suspended or terminated
- Account being banned

### 7. Rule Changes

7.1 **Right to Modify**: Kaitu reserves the right to modify these retailer rules at any time.

7.2 **Change Notification**: Rule changes will be communicated to retailers via email or website announcement.

7.3 **Continued Participation**: Continuing to participate in the retailer program after rule changes indicates acceptance of the new rules.
